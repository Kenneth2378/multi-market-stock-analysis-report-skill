# Legacy Report Content Contract

Use this contract as the minimum content standard. Preserve the analytical depth of the original report while applying current numeric, sourcing, language, semantic, and privacy controls.

## Required sections and items

### Core conclusion

- State latest complete close and daily move.
- Compare with the recent high/low and quantify drawdown or interval position.
- Give a company-specific comprehensive judgment.
- State what evidence would confirm recovery and what would indicate continued deterioration.

### Fundamentals: six items

1. Business model and main operating asset/subsidiary.
2. Earnings power: annual revenue, attributable profit, and operating cash flow with growth.
3. Growth: latest-quarter revenue, attributable profit, and adjusted profit with growth.
4. Earnings quality: gross margin, net margin, product mix, expenses, and one-off effects.
5. Solvency and asset quality: debt ratio, receivables, inventory, financing, and cash conversion.
6. Governance and shareholder background: control, material subsidiaries, transactions, and governance implications.

### Valuation: five items

1. Current market cap and PE/PB/PS or suitable alternatives.
2. Metric-basis conflicts and independently recalculated values.
3. Relative industry position using at least two comparable companies when available.
4. Recent or historical valuation/price position.
5. Growth-to-valuation match and conditions that would compress or support valuation.

### Technical and public price-volume inference: five items

1. Trend and phase structure.
2. Latest-day price, range, amount, turnover, and change.
3. Public capital-flow statistics when available, labeled as source-defined inference.
4. Interpretation of high-volume up/down days and whether capital confirmation is strengthening or weakening.
5. Concrete support, repair, confirmation, and pressure reference zones derived from observed prices and moving averages.

### News and sentiment: four items

1. Short-term market or sector impact.
2. Material company catalyst and impact mechanism.
3. Institutional/capital/crowd sentiment using dated evidence where available.
4. Sudden-event risks tied to the target company's earnings or valuation.

### Research judgment and methodology

- Comprehensive judgment.
- Three explicit evidence-chain paragraphs: fundamentals, valuation, and price/volume or capital behavior.
- Suitable research scenario.
- Upside, base, and downside scenarios with measurable triggers.
- Observation conditions.
- Invalidation conditions.
- Methodology explaining evidence weights.

### Risks and tracking

- List the financial and operating metrics to track in the next report.
- List support, repair, confirmation, and pressure reference zones.
- Include sources and disclaimer.

## Validation

Do not award PASS by keyword count alone. Confirm every required item contains target-specific facts, numbers, interpretation, and a source trail. A report shorter or less substantive than the supplied legacy benchmark must be `NEEDS_REVIEW` even if headings exist.
